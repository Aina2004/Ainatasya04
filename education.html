Primary School

The picture above is my primary school which is Sk Kampong Pasir Puteh. I attended school here since I was 6 years old, which is in 2010. This is because this school has a pre-school like a kindergarten. After that I continued my schooling from grade 1 to grade 6 at this school. This school is near of my old house, less than 10 minutes from home.

Secondary School

The picture above is my high school which is Smk Kampong Pasir Puteh. I started school here since I was 13 years old, form 1 and ended with form 5, "PT3" and "SPM" in this school is an unforgettable memory. The teachers at this school are very kind and caring about their students.

Diploma Level

After that, I continued my studies to the diploma level. I got an offer to study at UiTM Kedah branch and I got a course in information management for 2 and a half years (5 semesters) and this is my last semester in the diploma. I am happy and grateful that I am still given the opportunity to continue my studies with the support of my parents. There are too many memories, experiences and especially knowledge that I was able to learn during almost two and a half years at UiTM Kedah. Hopefully all the knowledge learned in the diploma can be used in the future.
