My Experience while Internship

One of my experience that I want to share is about the experience and journey that I gained during my internship. I did an internship during the 4th semester break which was around two months at the Ipoh City Council. At MBI I was an intern in the treasury department (system unit). Although I'm not skilled in the field of systems, but when I was an intern there, I got a lot of knowledge from their staff. Not only that, in the treasury department I also did document filing work because filing is one of my subject in previous sem.

In addition, I was also given the task of designing and editing posters and documentation using the Canva application. At the intern place, I was also given the task of creating a website for my department's incoming and outgoing letters with dhiya, my friend during the intern. This was a very challenging experience for me because I didn't learn how to make a website yet but dhiya and the staff taught me slowly how to create a website in basic. I really appreciate it.
